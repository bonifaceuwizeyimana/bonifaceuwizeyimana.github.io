About Me
April 13, 2025

Boniface Uwizeyimana (Member, IEEE) is currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Calgary. He obtained both his M.Sc. and B.Sc. degrees (with honours) in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Egypt-Japan University of Science and Technology (E-JUST) in 2024 and the University of Rwanda in 2019, respectively. His research interests encompass 5G, 6G, and future wireless technologies, including digital twins, machine learning applications in wireless communication, cognitive radio technology, non-terrestrial networks, and digital signal processing.
